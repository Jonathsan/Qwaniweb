QWANI TRUST
Qwani is a youth lead initiative created as a platform for young talented writers.
We are writers' forum that tries to expose people to
the beauty in the diverse forms of writing from young
fresh minds. As Qwani, we believe in diversity; thus,
we use our platform to showcase writing in the many
forms it takes, from short stories and poems to
plays and philosophy, along with semblances of different
languages; we try to allow all forms of
writters to show their knowledge in the specific
fields. We aim to inform, entertain, evoke emotion,
and most importantly, expose; the world to great writers and
amazing writers to the world.
